Overview

In the previous two sessions, we got an overview of how to work with Linked Data in Javascript and with Solid.

In this session, we will finish with an hands-on exercise.

Hands-on exercise

Send me a notification (https://pod.rubendedecker.be/profile/card#me).

Tell me who you are.

Let us know what you found of the past four days!

Linked Data Notifiations

For this, you should make use of the Linked Data Notifications specification. This is the supported specification for sending notifications to Solid pods.

Dont just read the full spec, focus on the useful parts.

Discovering the inbox

The first step is for you to discover where you should send your notification to. The goal is to send it to the inbox that is advertised by my WebID!

My WebID can be found at https://pod.rubendedecker.be/profile/card#me.

tip: look in the document itself, not in the headers

Creating the notification

Next, create the notification using Linked Data.

Feel free to use the tools shown in the previous sessions, or do it manually!

Use any data format you want, and if possible check it using the online playgrounds for turtle and JSON-LD!

Notification contents

The notification should contain at least the following predicates from the Activity Streams vocabulary:

- a type of as:Note.
- an as:actor predicate containing your WebID that has a foaf:name property set.
- an as:content predicate telling us who you thought of the past four days!

if you do not have a WebID, you can create one here.

Sending the notification

Now send me the notification you have made!

I will list all received notifications on screen using Comunica.

I am here for all your questions. Feel free to ask or discuss with me!
